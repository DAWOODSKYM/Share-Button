Ingredients

Mimosa Drink – a popular fruity cocktail made with a champagne and orange juice; this time I infused it with with pineapple puree. A must-have on , Mother’s Day or basically any special day!
Course: Drinks
Cuisine: American
Servings: 5 - 7 cups
Ingredients
3-4 cups orange juice (fresh squeezed) , chilled
1-2 cups pineapple Puree or juice , chilled
1 (750 ml) sparkling wine or champagne , chilled
¼ -½ cup rum or orange liqueur (optional)
Garnish: Strawberries, Orange slices

Method

Instructions Combine and pour orange juice, pineapple puree and and champagne in a pitcher. Then add add liquor to taste. This is optional, when serving for breakfast I omit the liquor. Fill champagne flutes with prepared mimosa. Garnish with sliced strawberry and or pineapple slices . Tips and Notes: Freshly squeezed orange juice is preferred as it is lighter, more delicate with a bit of tart than anything you buy in the store. The same goes with pineapple, too. But if you opt to buy orange juice at the store, go for a high-quality and not the concentrated ones. You can use any member of the orange family – from clementines, navel oranges to blood oranges. It’ll be fine. Use a dry sparkling wine instead of the sweet kind as they mix well with juice. Start with chilled ingredients as they yield far better tasting mimosas.

Ingredients

Grilling the lemons for lemonade adds a subtle smoky hint to the most summery of drinks. And the coconut cream adds a tropical taste. The two simple changes take this tasty refresher to the next level.
Ingredients
6 lemons, halved
1/2 cup coconut cream
15 cups water
1/2 to 3/4 cups sugar, to taste
Lemon slices

Method

1. Prepare the grill to medium-high heat.
2. On the direct heat of the grill place the lemons, sliced side down. Grill until caramelized and slightly charred.
3. In a gallon pitcher, squeeze the lemon juice. Add the coconut cream and water. Stir and sweeten to taste with sugar. Pour over ice to serve and serve with lemon slices.
